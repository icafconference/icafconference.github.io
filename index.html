About the ICAF 2020

Accounting and Finance are inseparable twin drivers of eﬃcient Economic System and thriving enterprises of all forms. The proposed conference aims to take stock of extant practices and research in these disciplines and the challenges they face and pose to the stability of the enterprises and the economic system these are supposed to subserve. The speciﬁc objectives of the conference are as follows :

  • To deliberate upon the role of Accounting & Finance in the emerging context of Anti globalisation, Nationalism and Sustainable Development Goals
  • To delve deeper on Accounting & Finance in the Age of Technology
  • To discuss Regulation, Risks, and Accountability in Accounting & Finance
  • To gain insights on the state of the art of and emerging trends in Financial Products, Markets and Institutions
  • To update one's knowledge on Educational Perspectives in Accounting & Finance in terms of strategies for enabling, engaging, empowering and enchanting the learners in these courses
  • To develop prowess in Accounting and Financial Analysis

About Indian Accounting Association

The Indian Accounting Association is a professional body of academics and practitioners in accounting and ﬁnance. In existence since 1970, the IAA has a network of 53 branches in India with more than 5600 life members, and a Research Foundation as an aﬃliate at Kolkata. It brings out a biannual research journal Indian Journal of Accounting in the months of June & December. The Association has instituted Annual IAA Young Research Awards and IAA Fellowships. The Association oﬀers Life Membership and Annual Membership to Individuals and Institutions through its chapters across India. IAA conferences in the past have attracted a large number of delegates from across the country and abroad.

Abstract and Paper submission Guidelines

  • A 500-word abstract, titled as Abstract to include Topic, Method, and Results, should be submitted via https://easychair.org/cfp/icaf2020.
  • Abstracts will be double-blind reviewed.
  • All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
  • The text must be in Times New Roman Font, Size 12, 1-inch margin on all sides, with headings in bold.
  • Papers will be evaluated by members of the Board of Reviewers and selected for publication.
